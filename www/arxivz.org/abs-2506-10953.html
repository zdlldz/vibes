Title: Toward a Unified Thermodynamic Model of VIBE

Authors: Prof. Aurum K. Flux, Dr. Ima Gine, and Dept. of Applied Chill

View PDF | HTML (experimental)

Abstract

We formalize VIBE as a coupled, bounded system with four measurable dimensions: Vibrations, Intimacy, Bureaucracy, and Entropy. Using an ad hoc but highly polished instrument stack, we report statistically suggestive evidence that VIBE signatures can be measured, normalized, and publicly argued about.
