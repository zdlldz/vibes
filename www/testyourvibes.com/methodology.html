VIBE Scoring Methodology (Aligned to arXivz:2502.VIBE01)

Inputs (normalised)

  • Vibrations (V): 0 to 1
  • Intimacy (I): 0 to 1
  • Bureaucracy Freedom (B): 0 to 1 (higher means less institutional friction)
  • Entropy (E): 0 to 1

Formula

score = (V * I * B * E)^(1/4)

The calculator clamps all user inputs to the 0-10 range, then divides by 10 to produce the [0, 1] values above.

Interpretation Bands

  • 0.00 - 0.25: vibes are critically misaligned
  • 0.26 - 0.60: vibes are unstable but salvageable
  • 0.61 - 0.80: vibes are coherent
  • 0.81 - 1.00: vibes are immaculate
