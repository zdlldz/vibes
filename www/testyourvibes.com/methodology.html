VIBE Scoring Methodology v0.1

Inputs

  • Vibrations (V)
  • Intimacy (I)
  • Bureaucracy (B)
  • Entropy (E)

Formula

score = (V + I + (10 - B) + (10 - E)) / 40

All terms are clamped to the 0-10 range before calculation.

Interpretation Bands

  • 0.00 - 0.32: vibes are critically misaligned
  • 0.33 - 0.66: vibes are negotiable
  • 0.67 - 1.00: vibes are immaculate
