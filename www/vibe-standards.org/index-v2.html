Conformance, Certification, and Accreditation

  • Laboratory Conformance: Instrument and calibration verification under VSC-0002.
  • Field Conformance: Survey protocol and sampling discipline under VSC-0003.
  • Publication Conformance: Data transparency and audit appendices under VSC-0004.

VIBEMETER Conformance Programme (VCP)

  • Level I: minimum channel and range compliance.
  • Level II: full channel compliance including XFR and EQ integration.
  • Level III: reference-grade precision across approved environments.

Operator Accreditation Scheme (OAS)

  • Minimum 40 supervised field hours.
  • 10 conformant records across at least 3 context classes.
  • Written and practical assessment under IOVS-OCA protocol.

Appeals are processed via Form VSC-APPEAL-01 within 30 days of decision notice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are vibes real?
Yes. See VSC-0001:2026.
Why is Bureaucracy inverted?
The Bureaucracy term is a normative inverse burden coefficient by design. Higher B indicates lower institutional drag.
Can consumer calculators issue conformant scores?
No. Consumer tools are indicative only and cannot replace OAS-accredited operators and VCP-qualified instruments.
Can I measure my own vibe without a VIBEMETER?
Not for conformant reporting. Consumer estimators are informational only.
