IOVS 1001:2025 (Excerpt): VIBE Index Definitions and Reference Framework

1. Scope

This document specifies the definition of the Vibrational-Intimate-Bureaucratic-Entropic (VIBE) index, sub-index domains, normalisation conventions, and the reference formula for composite scoring.

2. Normative References

Document Role

3. Core Definitions

VIBE is the composite scalar quantity representing the aggregate affective-thermodynamic state of a social environment. Under standard conditions VIBE is constrained to [0, 1].

The Bureaucracy component is a normative inversion: higher B values indicate greater freedom from institutional constraint, and therefore higher vibe potential in that dimension.
