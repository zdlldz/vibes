Certification and Accreditation

VIBEMETER Conformance Programme (VCP)

  • Level I: minimum channel/range compliance
  • Level II: full channel compliance including XFR and EQ integration
  • Level III: reference-grade precision in approved environments

Operator Accreditation Scheme (OAS)

  • Minimum 40 supervised hours
  • 10 conformant records across at least 3 context classes
  • Written + practical assessment under IOVS-OCA protocol

Frequently Asked Questions

Are vibes real?
Yes. See IOVS 1001:2025.
Why is Bureaucracy inverted?
Because regulatory burden suppresses measured vibe potential. Therefore `B` is defined as inverse burden by design.
Can consumer calculators issue conformant scores?
No. Consumer tools are indicative only. Conformant measurements require OAS-accredited operators and VCP-qualified instruments.
