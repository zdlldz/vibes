Open Standards for Vibe Measurement

Interoperable protocols for the rigorous quantification of Vibrational-Intimate-Bureaucratic-Entropic phenomena.

Mission

The VSC develops and maintains open standards so VIBE instruments can exchange data across labs, events, and lifestyle platforms without sacrificing methodological rigour or ceremonial integrity.

Current Standard

VSC-0001:2026 — VIBE Index: Definitions, Units, and Reference Framework — specifies the four sub-indices (V, I, B, E), composite scoring formulae, colloquial reference anchors, measurement conditions, and data reporting requirements for conformant VIBE measurements. Second edition; supersedes VSC-0001:2023.
